I lost my matric card, how do I get a new matric card?

For new students, you can go to Pejabat Pendaftar UTHM to create your matric card.

For current students, if you redo the matric card due to loss, you will be fined RM50 for the first time, RM100 for the second time, and then stacked. Up to 4 times.
  i)   Firstly, you need to go to Pejabat Keselamatan UTHM.
       (It around Pejabat Pendaftar UTHM)
  ii)  Secondly, you need to make a report about the missing matric card.
  iii) Thirdly, you need to go to Pejabat Bendahari UTHM to pay your fine.
  iv) Lastly, you need to go to Pejabat Pendaftar UTHM to create your        new matric card.

Do I need to bring my own transportation?

For Year 1 students, you cannot bring your own transportation yet, only Year 2 and above can bring their own transportation. However, you can use buses provided by UTHM to go to classes and around the campus. Thus, bringing your own vehicle is not necessary.

How do I pay my fee?

Payment needs to be made online thus you can go to https://epayment.uthm.edu.my to settle your fees.

What are the tools for learning UTHM ?

UTHM uses Author and SMAP platform for the staff and students. Author is the platform for you to receive and submit your tasks. It’s a dual communication with your lecturer about your class and taks. Next, SMAP is the platform for all students and staff to get new information, attendance for your class, register stickers, courses, to check and pay your fees and others.
